About Us

CHRISTMAS CHARITY

Secret Santa for Kids - is a gift giving project by unknown people to children. We are volunteer-team who help children from low-income families.

BECOME SECRET SANTA

All you need - just sign up on the site. Our team call to you and send list of presrnts which children asked in letters.

HOW CAN YOU HELP?

If you love the positivity and joy that Santa Claus brings to kids, join to our team and send the children the gifts of their dreams on Christmas this year.

HOW DOES IT WORK ON SITE?

You can decide what do you want to buy and pay for it. Our team will buy, pack and deliver gifts to children whose parents have contacted us.
