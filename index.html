About Us

CHRISTMAS CHARITY

Secret Santa for Kids - is a gift giving project by unknown people to children. We are a team of volunteers who help children from low-income families.

HOW TO BECOME SECRET SANTA?

All you need - just sign up on the site. Our team call to you and send list of presrnts which children asked in letters.

HOW CAN YOU HELP?

Join us and send the children the gift of their dreams.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

You can decide what do you want to buy and pay for it. Our team will buy, pack and deliver gifts to children whose parents have contacted us.
